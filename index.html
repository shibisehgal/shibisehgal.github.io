Hello Internet!

I'm Shibani Sehgal. I'm an artist, illustrator and art experimentalist. I endeavour to express the emotions of nature through the use of soft pastels, pen-and-ink, collage work and large canvas work. My works have been featured in various groups and solo exhibitions, both domestic and international. My works are in collection of various eminent personalities including the Ex Vice President of India.

I graduated from College of Art (1992), New Delhi with a Masters in History of Art from the National Museum Institute (1995). I was awarded the gold medal by the National Museum Institute for my research on the works.

As an artist, illustrator and art experimentalist, I exhibit versatility and flexibility in my continuing adventure with colors and various media. I hope to inspire everyone to look towards art as a means of expression that transcends language and extends into the realm of unfettered freedom of exploration.
